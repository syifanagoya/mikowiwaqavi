    The Italian MTV Awards are fast approaching and many international artists from around the world have been chosen as lucky nominees! Some international categories include Best Band, Best Male, Best Female, as well as Best New Artist.

    Best International Band nominees include the multi-platinum selling groups Little Mix, One Direction,  Coldplay and 5 Seconds of Summer! A BIG category at this annual Italian MTV Awards. This also goes for Best New Artist, where Lukas Graham, Joan Thiele, Benji & Fede, and Shawn Mendes were the chosen nominees.

    All you have to do is click here, and you’ll be directed to the main voting site. After that, you simply pick the category you want to choose, followed by the artist you’d like to vote for and you’re good to go! You can vote as many times as you want so make sure you vote, vote, vote!

    You can also share the site on your Facebook and Twitter accounts so you can get your friends to pick and choose their favorite artists to win as well.

    Voting ends soon, so get your clicking ready! On your marks, get set, GO!

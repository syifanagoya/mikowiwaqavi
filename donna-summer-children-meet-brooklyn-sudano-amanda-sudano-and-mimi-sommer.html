    Donna Summer is survived by three daughters who are Mimi Sommer, Brooklyn Sudano, and Amanda Sudano.

    Mimi Sommer, born on 16th January 1973, is Donna Summer’s eldest daughter from her first marriage to Helmut Sommer.

    Mimi Sommer followed in her mother’s footsteps and pursued a career in the music industry. She has showcased her talent as a singer-songwriter and has released her own music.

    Brooklyn Sudano, born on 5th January 1981, is Donna Summer’s second daughter from her marriage to Bruce Sudano.

    Following her parents’ creative paths, Brooklyn Sudano ventured into acting. She has appeared in various television series and films, including “My Wife and Kids” and “Cuts.”

    Amanda Sudano, born on 11th August 1982, is Donna Summer’s youngest child and Bruce Sudano’s second daughter.

    Like her mother and sister Mimi, Amanda also pursued a career in music. She formed the musical duo Johnnyswim with her husband, Abner Ramirez. Johnnyswim has gained recognition for its folk, soul, and pop-infused music, and Amanda’s powerful vocals have contributed to its success.

    Donna Summer’s children have carried on her musical legacy in their own unique ways. They have found success and artistic fulfillment in the entertainment industry, following in the footsteps of their talented mother.

    Despite the loss of Donna Summer, her children continue to honor her memory and make their mark in the world of music and entertainment.

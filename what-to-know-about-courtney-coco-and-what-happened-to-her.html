    Courtney Coco was a 19 year old student from Rapides Parish, Louisiana. She was enrolled in the criminal justice program at Northwestern State University. Information has it that Stephanie Belgard, Courtney’s mother, last saw her on October 1, 2004.

    Her mother recounted to Dateline NBC. She said “It was opening day of squirrel season and we were going camping for the weekend. Courtney was over at my house and I asked her if she wanted to go with us.”

    “But she didn’t want to go. She’s not really the camping type.”

    “She always worked hard to better herself and build her future,” Stephanie Belgard said of Courtney Coco.

    “She just loved life. Her sisters and nephews miss her greatly.”

    “We’re a close-knit family and it’s been extremely tough for them. For all of us.”

    A week ago, Dodd told her Instagram followers that her boyfriend, whose identity was unknown, had just dumped her. But the two have apparently got back together since then – and we now know who he is. The RHOC star just shared a photo of her posing with her boyfriend, whose name in Brian Reagan.

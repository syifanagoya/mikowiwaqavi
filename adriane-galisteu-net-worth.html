    What is Adriane Galisteu's Net Worth?

    Adriane Galisteu is a Brazilian model, actress, and television host who has a net worth of $10 million dollars. Adriane Galisteu was born in Sao Paulo, Brazil in April 1973. Her father passed away when she was 15 and her brother passed away at 28 from AIDS. Adriane has been in the Brazilian edition of Playboy magazine many times and was on the cover in August 1995 and August 2011. She worked as a VJ for MTV Brasil. She was dating Formula One driver Ayrton Senna at the time of his death from a racing accident in 1994. Her first acting appearance acme in the TV series Antonio Alves, Taxista in 1996.

    She appeared in episodes of Xica da Silva, Sai de Baixo, and Fascinacao. Her first film role came in the 2005 movie Chick Thing as Mayara. She also starred as Marina in the 2009 movie If I Were You 2. She has hosted many television shows including Projecto Fashion, Mutio+, and Agora e Tarde. She authored the book O Caminho das Borboletas about her relationship with Senna. She has also dated the Brazilian soccer player Roger Galera Flores. Adriane has a son with Alexandre Lodice.

    Chloe Melas has covered the numerous startling stories on CNN. She is known for her investigative reporting on the sexual allegations of top celebrities Harvey Weinstein, Kevin Spacey and Morgan Freeman.

    Chloe Melas is an American journalist currently serving as an entertainment reporter for CNN. Based on CNN’s New York bureau, she covers entertainment and Hollywood for the network across platforms.

    Chloe Melas Wiki; Age, Birthday, Family, Education

    Chloe Elizabeth Melas was born in 1986 in Atlanta, Georgia, United State. By her age, she is 34 and her birthday falls on 11 June. She is the daughter of George Melas and Elizabeth Murphy-Melas.

    Her father is an architect and her mother Elizabeth is the author of children’s books. Elizabeth has published numerous books such as Watching Bradley Grow (1995), Pennies, Nickles & Dimes (1999), The Girl with No Hair (2001), and Just Like Grandpa (2011). Melas is Greek-American by ethnicity. She has a younger brother named Stefan Melas.

    Chloe attended Auburn University in Auburn, Alabama. There, she earned her bachelors degree in Broadcast Journalism with a major in in Radio, TV & Film.

    Her Career; CNN, HollywoodLife, VH1

    Chloe began her career at the age of 22 as a news assistant on CNN. After working for a year, she left the station in 2009 after meeting editor Bonnie Fuller, helping her launch Hollywood Life. There, she worked as a senior reporter for nearly seven years, writing numerous entertainment articles.

    From 2013 to 2015, Melas appeared as one of the hosts of VH1’s morning show, The Gossip Table. Afterward, she joined the New York City television station WPIX-TV and worked as a contributor until 2016. Besides, she served as a contributor to numerous programs including Fox & Friends, HLN, The Wendy Williams Show, The Insider, and The Today Show.

    In 2016, she joined CNN and started working as an Entertainment reporter. She now covers the entertainment and Hollywood for the network based in New York. Talking about her salary, she earns an average salary of $44700 per year as a CNN reporter.

    Who Is Chloe Melas’s Husband?

    On her personal side, the 34-year-old is married to her longterm love Brian Mazza. They first met on a blind date in New York City in 2012 and began their relationship. After dating for years, they got engaged in July 2013. The duo exchanged the wedding vows on 25 October 2014 in New York City.

    Brian is a businessman by profession, who is the founder and CEO of High Performance Lifestyle Training, based in New York. Besides, he is the president of Paige Hospitality Group and a creator of an athletic apparel brand, MAZZA SPORT. He is a graduate of the University Of Rhode Island with a BA in Communications.

    Welcomed Two Sons via IVF

    Chloe and her husband Brian are the parents of two sons. Their first baby, a son Leo DeSales Mazza was born on 3 July 2017. Similarly, she gave birth to a second son Luke Alessandro Mazza on 5 July 2019.

    They conceived both their first and second babies via in vitro fertilization (IVF) because of a diagnosis of poor ovarian reserve and low sperm count. Initially, they turned to medicated intrauterine insemination (IUI) to attempt to start their family but did not succeed after three rounds of the treatment.  Later, they decided in vitro fertilization (IVF) procedure, which became successful in conceiving both babies.
